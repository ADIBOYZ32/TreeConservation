🌿 Daily Eco Tasks (Day 1 - 7) 🌎

  • Day 1:♻️Segregate your household waste into dry and wet waste.
  • Day 2:🌊Save water — fix leaks and turn off taps while brushing.
  • Day 3:🌳Plant a tree or water a plant near your home.
  • Day 4:🚯Avoid single-use plastics; use reusable bags and bottles.
  • Day 5:💡Turn off electrical devices when not in use to save energy.
  • Day 6:🚴‍♂️Use a bicycle or walk instead of using a vehicle.
  • Day 7:📢Spread awareness — talk to a friend or post about conservation.
